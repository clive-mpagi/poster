Love With Balance by Clive

For everyone who loves deeply and wants to stay whole

When you love someone, give from strength, not from emptiness. Let your care be steady, your respect absolute, and your boundaries clear for both of you.

Today I will love her with honesty and respect, but I won't forget myself.

I will speak kindly, listen carefully, and never pressure anyone.

I will give love, not out of fear, but out of peace. I will take care of my heart and my dreams.

Read this every morning. A strong you is the best gift you can give the person you love.

Love her gently. Respect her fully. Care for yourself daily.I love you Quin!
